About ORAÉ

ORAÉ Health is redefining personalised haircare by combining professional expertise, cultural understanding, and intelligent technology.

Haircare guidance today is often generic and disconnected from real lived experience. Many individuals struggle with product damage, inconsistent routines, and advice that does not reflect their hair type, lifestyle, or cultural needs.

ORAÉ was created to change this. Built on real salon knowledge and supported by data-driven intelligence, ORAÉ helps users understand their hair health, avoid unnecessary damage, and follow routines that are tailored to them not trends.

ORAÉ Health was founded by Tumininu, bringing together professional haircare insight and a deep understanding of cultural hair needs. ORAÉ exists to empower individuals with clarity, confidence, and intelligent guidance they can trust throughout their haircare journey.

Why AI Matters in Haircare

Hair is not static. It changes with environment, health, routine, and time. Yet most haircare advice remains fixed, generic, and disconnected from lived experience.

ORAÉ uses artificial intelligence to recognise patterns across hair texture, scalp condition, lifestyle, and outcomes. Instead of one-size-fits-all advice, recommendations evolve as the user’s hair responds and grows.

By learning from real results and professional haircare knowledge, ORAÉ delivers guidance that becomes more accurate, more personal, and more effective over time.

AI allows haircare to become responsive, adaptive, and truly personal.
